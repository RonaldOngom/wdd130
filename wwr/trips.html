Available Rafting Trips

Beginner Rapids

Perfect for first-timers and families, this experience offers a welcoming introduction to white-water rafting. You’ll glide through gentle, friendly rapids that ripple like playful waves rather than roaring walls of water, allowing everyone to relax and enjoy the journey. Along the way, the river opens up to breathtaking scenic views lush riverbanks, open skies, and the quiet rhythm of nature moving at its own pace. Our expert guides are with you every stroke of the paddle, sharing clear instructions, safety tips, and fascinating stories about the river and its surroundings. Their calm presence turns uncertainty into confidence, making even beginners feel like seasoned adventurers. This trip isn’t just about the rapids it’s about laughter echoing across the water, shared moments with loved ones, and creating memories that flow as smoothly as the river itself.

Intermediate Challenge

A perfect blend of exhilaration and mastery, this adventure offers adventurers the thrill of stronger rapids while maintaining a sense of control. Navigate twisting currents and playful waves with confidence, guided by our skilled experts who ensure each rapid delivers excitement without compromising safety.

Advanced Extreme

Designed for thrill-seekers. Experience powerful rapids with our most experienced guides.
